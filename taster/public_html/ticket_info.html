-- Snowball Ticketing Information --
Tickets for Selwyn Snowball 2017 will go on sale on Saturday 28th October. Selwyn students can buy tickets from 10am with general release to all Cambridge students at 11am. (Selwyn Students must use their Cambridge email to access the system). The link to purchase tickets will be shared on our Facebook page and on our website. Please note that payment will be required immediately in order to secure tickets. Tickets include unlimited drinks and food, alongside spectacular entertainment and incredible music. Ticket prices are:
- General £85 (+ booking fee)
- VIP £95 (+ booking fee)
VIP tickets include a champagne reception in Selwyn College Chapel and early entry to the Ball. You are limited to purchase a maximum of either:
- 2x VIP tickets
- 4x General tickets
If this limitation presents an issue, please contact: ticketing@selwynsnowball.co.uk

-- Payment Information --
To buy your tickets to this year's Snowball, you can pay by two different methods.
1) you can pay using the Lydia payments app or,
2) a credit/debit card.
Unlike previous years, payment will be required immediately.
You must have a UK phone number and UK bank account to pay using the Lydia app.
To pay by card you must have a Visa/Mastercard from the following countries: Austria, Belgium, Canada, Croatia, Denmark, Finland, France, Germany, Greece, Ireland, Italy, Luxembourg, Malta, Monaco, Norway, Netherlands, Portugal, Spain, Sweden, UK. If your card country is not supported, please make sure that you let us know in advance by contacting ticketing@selwynsnowball.co.uk
