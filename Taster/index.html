Selwyn Snowball

The only annual Winter Ball at The University Of Cambridge

Every year, Selwyn College is transformed into a magical venue, bursting with musical performances, riveting activities, and of course, limitless supplies of food and drinks. The Snowball has become the most highly anticipated event of Cambridge’s Michaelmas term, and we can’t wait to share what we have planned for the 2017 event.

Work

Every year Snowball recruits dozens of workers, to ensure the event runs as smoothly as possible. We are looking for all types of workers, from security personnel to bar staff. For the first time ever, we will also be recruiting several Area Managers, to help the Committee during the night. Information on these roles and the application process will be released in due course.
